Biography

Morgan Jeremiah, professionally known as Aviator Violinist, is a Nigerian violinist and aviation student known for expressive performances that blend elegance, excellence, and emotion.

A versatile performer, he delivers beautiful violin music across multiple genres including classical, gospel, pop, blues, love songs, and contemporary/trending sounds.

Morgan performs at weddings, birthdays, proposals, church services, private events, and entertainment functions, creating memorable musical experiences for every audience.

Beyond music, his discipline and passion are shaped by his background in leadership, service, and aviation. Whether on stage or in the skies, dedication defines his journey.
